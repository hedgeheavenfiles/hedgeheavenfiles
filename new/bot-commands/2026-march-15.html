Skeletron
Skeletron is a pre-Hardmode boss. Defeating Skeletron for the first time grants unlimited access to all Dungeon areas for players within the current world and allows the Clothier NPC to move into an available vacant house. If attempting to enter the Dungeon before killing Skeletron, Dungeon Guardians spawn once a player reaches 0 depth. These enemies deal 1,000 damage and generally kill any player in a single hit.
